BlackBerry, formally known as Research in Motion Limited (RIM), is a Canadian owned and operated company based out of Waterloo, Ontario, since 1984. BlackBerry designs and produces various types of software, hardware and service solutions for business and personal consumers all over the world. BlackBerry is well known for producing the BlackBerry smart phone, introduced in 1999, which has been very successful on the global market. Other products and software produced include the recently released Z10 and Q10 smartphones, the Playbook tablet, BlackBerry Messenger, App World, and numerous Operating Systems.

Quick Facts

  • Formed in 1984
  • Headquarters in Waterloo, Ontario
  • Offices across the globe. ie. North America, Asia-Pacific and Europe
  • Released the BlackBerry smartphone (1999)
  • President and CEO Thorsten Heins

Introduction

My name is Stephanie Worsfold, I am currently enrolled at the University of Guelph and am about to complete my last semester of school in a Bachelor of Computing degree, specializing in Computer Science. I have also completed all of the required Co-op work terms for my degree. Through working with the Co-op program at the University, I have had the opportunity to apply my knowledge from the classroom in the industry. I plan to explore many areas of my major, but right now I have an interest in expanding my knowledge in app development, automated testing, web development, image processing, algorithms, and more modular programming in general.

I am also pursuing a minor in marketing management, so I have a keen interest in the business related aspects of companies as well.

I hope this website provides you with a glimpse of what it is like to have the opportunity to work in a computing based position at these companies, and perhaps encourages you to explore a similar path if you are in this field.

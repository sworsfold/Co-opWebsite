Job Description

My role at PerkinElmer was Associate Software QA Engineer. I was testing a PerkinElmer product called iLAB. My position allowed me to gain experience testing on VMs and multiple tablets. As my term progressed I was able to contribute to the creation of an automated testing framework for iLAB. I was responsible for creating test cases and framework features to support a smoke test workflow for two modules of the product. I enjoyed this aspect of the role as I was able to develop my programming skills. With the automated tasks came an opportunity to investigate third party services for different browser and platform testing.

    PerkinElmer

    PerkinElmer, Inc. is a multinational corporation that focuses on the business areas of human and environmental health. The company was founded in 1931 and today has over 7,000 employees. PerkinElmer is a $2.2 billion dollar company with a mission to improve the health and safety of people and the environment.

    Quick Facts:

    • Headquarters are in Waltham, MA
    • 7,600 employees worldwide
    • Presence in 150 countries across the world
    • CEO Robert Friel
    • 3,300 patents
